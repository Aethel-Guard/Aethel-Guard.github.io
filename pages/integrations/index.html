How data flows between products

1

Vigilar detects a threat on your device

On-device engines scan for credential breaches, tracking, phishing, and PII exposure. Event is classified and sent to Vigilar Guard via REST API.

2

Guard triages with AI

AI analyses the event, enriches it with threat intel (OpenCTI, MISP, AbuseIPDB), assigns severity, and generates a response recommendation.

3

Alert routes to your tools

Forwarded to your SIEM (Wazuh/Splunk/QRadar), incident platform (TheHive), and alerting channel (Slack/Teams/PagerDuty). Simultaneously sent back to Vigilar on the user's device.

4

AI SOC Team orchestrates response

For enterprise: AI agents coordinate containment, forensics, and remediation across the full SOC stack — TheHive, Shuffle, MISP, and OpenCTI.
