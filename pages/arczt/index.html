// Features

Security by architecture

Every feature designed around one principle: your data never leaves your device. No exceptions. No excuses.

🔐

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Your master password never leaves your device. We can't see your vault, we can't reset your password, we can't decrypt your data. Not even if we wanted to. Not even with a court order. The encryption keys are derived on your device.

⚛️

Quantum-Resistant Encryption

AES-256-GCM for authenticated encryption today. Post-quantum lattice-based cryptography layered on top for tomorrow. Your vault is protected against both classical and quantum attacks. Future-proof by design.

On-Device Only

Your vault lives on your device. Sync happens peer-to-peer between your devices — no cloud relay, no intermediary server. Your credentials never touch infrastructure we control. P1 redline: PII never leaves.

💻

Cross-Platform

// Encryption

Two layers. Zero compromises.

Classical encryption for today. Post-quantum encryption for tomorrow. Both layers, every credential, all the time.

🔐

How it works

  • ✓ Layer 1 — AES-256-GCM: Authenticated symmetric encryption. Your vault is encrypted with a key derived from your master password using Argon2id key derivation.
  • ✓ Layer 2 — Post-Quantum Lattice: Module-Lattice-Based KEM (ML-KEM-768) wraps the AES key. Even a quantum computer can't break it.
  • ✓ Key Derivation: Argon2id with high memory and iteration parameters. Resistant to GPU and ASIC attacks.
  • ✓ Peer-to-Peer Sync: Devices sync directly over end-to-end encrypted local network or Tor. No cloud relay.
  • ✓ Zero-Knowledge: We never receive your vault, your master password, or your encryption keys. By architecture.
