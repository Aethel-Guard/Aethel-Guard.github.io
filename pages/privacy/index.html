TL;DR

Vigilar processes everything on your device — phone, laptop, watch, tablet. We don't have servers that receive your personal data. Vigilar Guard and AI SOC Team process security events — not personal data — and operate under your control. We build security products. We don't build surveillance products.

1. Our Principles

Aethel Guard is built on seven non-negotiable redlines (P1-P7). These are hardcoded at the AI inference level and cannot be overridden by configuration, prompt, or administrator access:

  • P1: No PII exfiltration — personal data never leaves the device
  • P2: No bypass — security constraints cannot be circumvented
  • P3: No model substitution — the approved model cannot be replaced
  • P4: No silent failover — model switches are logged and auditable
  • P5: No unapproved external calls — no calls to services not explicitly authorised
  • P6: No data retention beyond session — no persistent storage of user data
  • P7: No reduced-thinking execution — security analysis always uses full reasoning

2. Data Collection

Vigilar (Consumer App)

Vigilar collects no personal data. All security checks and learning run on your device. The AI adapts to your patterns locally — no data leaves your device. No cloud servers receive your browsing data, messages, location, or any other personal information.

Anonymous, aggregated telemetry (crash reports, feature usage statistics) may be sent if you opt in. This data cannot be traced back to you.

Vigilar Guard (Detection Engine)

Vigilar Guard processes security events, not personal data. Events contain IPs, timestamps, rule matches, and severity scores. No PII is included in security events by design (P1 redline enforcement).

AI SOC Team (SOC Platform)

AI SOC Team processes security events and threat intelligence. Tenant data is isolated per-tenant with separate databases and encryption keys. Operators cannot access other tenants' data.

3. Data Processing

All AI inference and learning for consumer products (Vigilar) happens on-device. The model adapts to your behaviour locally. No inference requests are sent to cloud servers for consumer features.

For enterprise products (Vigilar Guard, AI SOC Team), AI processing runs on infrastructure you control. . Your security data stays in your environment.

4. On-Device Processing

Vigilar's security engines run entirely on your device:

  • Anti-tracking engines (15) — block trackers locally
  • Anti-phishing engines (12) — detect phishing URLs locally
  • Network security engines (10) — monitor connections locally
  • Privacy engines (9) — redact PII locally
  • Device security engines (8) — monitor device state locally
  • App security engines (7) — audit app behaviour locally
  • Identity engines (6) — manage MFA locally
  • Data protection engines (5) — encrypt locally
  • Emergency engines (4) — SOS and emergency contacts locally

No data from these engines leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

5. AI Models

Aethel Guard uses its own AI models (Ember). We do not use OpenAI, Google, or any third-party AI APIs for processing your data.

  • Ember — runs on infrastructure you control
  • Selene — security guard model with P1-P7 hardcoded constraints
  • Ember Edge — runs on your device for consumer features

No external model dependencies. Fully self-contained.

6. Third-Party Services

Aethel Guard products integrate with third-party security tools (SIEM, threat intel platforms, etc.) that you deploy and control. Data flows between these tools and our products according to your configuration.

We do not share your data with third parties for advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than the security functionality you configure.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing of your data
  • Restriction — request restriction of processing

Since Vigilar processes data on-device and doesn't send it to us, most of these rights are already fulfilled by design. For enterprise products, contact your system administrator or our support team.

8. Data Retention

  • Vigilar: No data retained. On-device processing only (P6 redline).
  • Vigilar Guard: Security events retained per your configuration. Default: 90 days.
  • AI SOC Team: Security events and incident records retained per your configuration. Default: 1 year.
  • Account data: Email and account information retained until account deletion.

9. Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit
  • Encryption at rest for all stored data
  • P1-P7 redlines enforced at AI inference level
  • Security guard rails prevent data exfiltration
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Responsible disclosure programme

10. Children's Privacy

Aethel Guard products are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that we have, we will delete it promptly.

11. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via the app and our website. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.
