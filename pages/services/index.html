SERVICE 01

Data Sovereignty Architecture

Design systems where your data stays yours — by architecture, not by policy. We map every data flow, identify sovereignty risks, and architect solutions that make compliance a structural guarantee.

🗺️ Data Residency Mapping

Full analysis of where your data lives, moves, and rests — across clouds, regions, and third parties.

  • Cloud region inventory and data flow mapping
  • Third-party data processor audit
  • Cross-border transfer risk assessment
  • Data classification and sensitivity tagging

🏛️ Compliance-by-Design

Architecture that makes GDPR, DORA, NIS2, and data localisation requirements structural — not procedural.

  • GDPR Article 25 data protection by design
  • DORA ICT risk management framework
  • NIS2 security measures and reporting
  • Sector-specific compliance (health, finance, energy)

🔐 Encryption Architecture

Key management, encryption at rest and in transit, and HSM integration — designed so that even we can't read your data.

  • End-to-end encryption architecture
  • HSM and key management design
  • Zero-knowledge proof integration
  • Homomorphic encryption assessment

🤝 Supplier Security Assessment

Evaluate every vendor and processor in your supply chain for data sovereignty risks — before they become incidents.

  • Vendor data handling questionnaire and audit
  • Sub-processor chain analysis
  • Contractual data protection review
  • Ongoing monitoring framework design

From €5,000 for a data sovereignty assessment

Full architecture engagements typically run 4-8 weeks. Every engagement includes a written report with prioritised recommendations and architecture diagrams.

Security System Redesign

🔄 SIEM Migration

Move from Splunk, QRadar, or ArcSight to Wazuh, Elastic, or a custom stack — without losing visibility.

From network perimeter to identity-first architecture — micro-segmentation, conditional access, and continuous verification.

  • Conditional access policy framework
  • Continuous verification and monitoring

🏗️ Legacy Hardening

  • Attack surface reduction and hardening baselines

  • Incident detection for legacy protocols

📊 Compliance Gap Remediation

Close the gaps between your current state and where you need to be — with architecture, not just documentation.

SERVICE 03

Incident Response & Forensics

When something's already happened. 4-hour SLA for initial response. We contain, investigate, and remediate — then harden so it doesn't happen again.

🚨 Rapid Containment

First 4 hours: isolate, contain, stop the bleeding. We deploy alongside your team or independently.

  • Network isolation and lateral movement blocking
  • Credential rotation and access revocation
  • Indicator of compromise (IoC) identification
  • Stakeholder communication templates

🔍 Digital Forensics

Find root cause, preserve evidence, build the timeline. AI-accelerated forensic analysis assists every engagement.

  • Evidence preservation and chain of custody
  • Memory and disk forensics
  • Log analysis and timeline reconstruction
  • AI-assisted anomaly detection

🛠️ Remediation

Fix what was broken. Patch entry points, close gaps, and verify containment is complete.

  • Root cause remediation plan
  • Vulnerability patching and hardening
  • Monitoring rule deployment
  • Verification testing

📋 Post-Incident Review

No blame, no theatre — just honest assessment and concrete hardening recommendations.

  • Blameless post-incident review facilitation
  • Timeline reconstruction report
  • Prioritised hardening recommendations
  • Monitoring and detection improvements

4-hour SLA · Retainer or per-incident

Incident response retainers start at €2,500/month. Per-incident engagements start at €15,000. We'd rather you call us before something happens.
