// What we do

Four phases of incident response

Every incident is different, but the discipline is the same: contain, investigate, remediate, review. Here's what each phase covers.

🚨 Rapid Containment

First 4 hours: isolate, contain, stop the bleeding. We deploy alongside your team or independently.

  • Network isolation and lateral movement blocking
  • Credential rotation and access revocation
  • Indicator of compromise (IoC) identification
  • Stakeholder communication templates

🔍 Digital Forensics

Find root cause, preserve evidence, build the timeline. AI-accelerated forensic analysis assists every engagement.

  • Evidence preservation and chain of custody
  • Memory and disk forensics
  • Log analysis and timeline reconstruction
  • AI-assisted anomaly detection

🛠️ Remediation

Fix what was broken. Patch entry points, close gaps, and verify containment is complete.

  • Root cause remediation plan
  • Vulnerability patching and hardening
  • Monitoring rule deployment
  • Verification testing

📋 Post-Incident Review

No blame, no theatre — just honest assessment and concrete hardening recommendations.

  • Blameless post-incident review facilitation
  • Timeline reconstruction report
  • Prioritised hardening recommendations
  • Monitoring and detection improvements
