// What we do

Four pillars of data sovereignty

Each engagement is tailored to your regulatory landscape, infrastructure, and risk profile. Here's what a typical data sovereignty architecture engagement covers.

🗺️ Data Residency Mapping

Full analysis of where your data lives, moves, and rests — across clouds, regions, and third parties.

  • Cloud region inventory and data flow mapping
  • Third-party data processor audit
  • Cross-border transfer risk assessment
  • Data classification and sensitivity tagging

🏛️ Compliance-by-Design

Architecture that makes GDPR, DORA, NIS2, and data localisation requirements structural — not procedural.

  • GDPR Article 25 data protection by design
  • DORA ICT risk management framework
  • NIS2 security measures and reporting
  • Sector-specific compliance (health, finance, energy)

🔐 Encryption Architecture

Key management, encryption at rest and in transit, and HSM integration — designed so that even we can't read your data.

  • End-to-end encryption architecture
  • HSM and key management design
  • Zero-knowledge proof integration
  • Homomorphic encryption assessment

🤝 Supplier Security Assessment

Evaluate every vendor and processor in your supply chain for data sovereignty risks — before they become incidents.

  • Vendor data handling questionnaire and audit
  • Sub-processor chain analysis
  • Contractual data protection review
  • Ongoing monitoring framework design
