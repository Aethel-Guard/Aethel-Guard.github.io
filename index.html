The journey

From threat to resolution

How Aethel Guard products work together — from individual protection to enterprise response.

Step 01 — Protect

Your device. Your rules.

Vigilar runs 76 security engines directly on your phone or desktop. Anti-tracking, MFA, PII redaction — all on-device. P1 redline: PII never leaves. No cloud. No exceptions.

On-device P1-P7 Redlines Zero Cloud 15 Anti-Tracking
Step 02 — Detect

AI sees what humans miss.

Vigilar Guard ingests security events, runs Sigma rules, triages with Ember AI, and enriches with threat intelligence. One engine. Every SIEM.

Sigma + AI Triage Wazuh · OpenCTI · MISP CEF/syslog
Step 03 — Respond

Four AI agents. Always on.

AI SOC Team runs Alpha (detect), Bravo (respond), Charlie (intel), Delta (forensics). Multi-tenant, GDPR compliant, Docker Compose deploy.

Alpha · Bravo · Charlie · Delta Multi-tenant GDPR
Step 04 — Intelligence

The brain behind every shield.

Ember Core powers every product. Ember Prime (12B) for inference, Selene for security guards hardcoded at inference, Ember Edge (3.1B) for on-device. No Ollama. No Gemma. Our own model.
